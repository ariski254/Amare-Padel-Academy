For the little ones, we offer three levels designed to foster a love of padel from an early age.

  • Mini-Padel (4-6 years): Perfect for kids just discovering the sport.
  • Kids Padel (7-10 years): Focuses on developing basic technical skills.
  • Advanced Kids Padel (10+ years): Focuses on enhancing techniques and introducing match play.

For adults, we offer four levels designed to cater to both beginners and advanced players.

  • Beginners: Focuses on fundamentals and court movements.
  • Bronze: Introduces light tactical play and builds on fundamentals.
  • Silver: Intermediate players refine their skills and strategies.
  • Gold: For advanced players looking to fine-tune their technique and competitive play.

We offer a comprehensive approach to competition-level training.

  • Physical Preparation: 3 to 5 days a week, depending on the age.
  • Training Hours: 3-5 days of training each week, tailored to age and experience.
  • Match Play: Mandatory participation in matches to sharpen skills.
  • Theory: 2-3 hours of theoretical lessons per week.
  • Match Analysis: In-depth analysis to improve tactics.
  • Nutrition: Specialized nutrition plans for competitive players from 4 years old.
